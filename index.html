Meet some of the best and most popular Formula 1 drivers

Ayrton Senna - 1960

Ayrton Senna da Silva was a Brazilian racing driver who won the Formula One World Drivers' Championship in 1988, 1990, and 1991.

Michael Schumacher - 1969

Michael Schumacher is a German former racing driver who competed in Formula One for Jordan, Benetton, Ferrari, and Mercedes.

Kimi Räikkönen - 1979

Kimi-Matias Räikkönen, nicknamed "The Iceman", is a Finnish racing driver who competed in Formula One between 2001 and 2021 for Sauber, McLaren, Ferrari, Lotus, and Alfa Romeo.

Charles Leclerc - 1997

Charles Marc Hervé Perceval Leclerc is a Monégasque racing driver, currently racing in Formula One for Scuderia Ferrari.

Sebastian Vettel - 1987

Sebastian Vettel is a German racing driver who competed in Formula One from 2007 to 2022 for BMW Sauber, Toro Rosso, Red Bull, Ferrari and Aston Martin.

Alain Prost - 1955

Alain Marie Pascal Prost is a retired French racing driver and Formula One team owner. A four-time Formula One World Drivers' Champion.
